Paige Marie Oamek is a writer, fact-checker, and aspiring editor in Brooklyn (by way of Iowa)

Their writing has appeared in The Nation, In These Times, The New Republic, The American Prospect, The Cleveland Review of Books, FAIR, and more.

Most recently she’s assisted the audience and breaking news team at The New Republic magazine. In the fall, Paige will pursue her master's at the CUNY Newmark School of Journalism. Paige additionally serves as podcast producer for The Bitchuation Room with Francesca Fiorentini and a researcher for The Drift magazine.

Paige writes about politics, homelessness, labor, and business with an eye toward technology’s role. She also co-hosts the podcast Unsheltered.
